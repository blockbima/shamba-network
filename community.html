Run a Node

Deploy the open-source SDK as a Docker container on the cloud. Fetch satellite data from NASA, ESA, NOAA, and JAXA, process it through open-source pipelines, and earn rewards for providing accurate climate data. Optimized for on-demand execution to keep costs low.

Coming Soon

Collect Field Data

Use the Shamba mobile app to collect geotagged photos, GPS coordinates, and field measurements (crop height, soil conditions, tree counts) that validate satellite data. Essential for carbon credit verification, biodiversity assessments, and land use validation.

Validate Data

Apply your domain expertise as an agronomist, ecologist, carbon analyst, or climate scientist. Review edge cases where satellite data and ground truth conflict, synthesize multiple data sources, and provide expert determinations.

Stake & Earn

Put your tokens to work by staking them with trusted data providers. Earn a share of rewards when providers you back are selected to serve data. Use the credibility ranking to choose providers with proven accuracy, uptime, and tenure.
