September 2023 — Laikipia & Kajiado Counties

Drought Insurance for Pastoralists

In partnership with Mercy Corps Ventures, Fortune Credit, and DIVA Technologies, Shamba deployed anticipatory cash transfers for 262 pastoralists across two Kenyan counties. Smart contracts held donated funds in escrow on Polygon, disbursing automatically when Shamba's oracle detected pasture conditions reaching distress levels via NDVI satellite data.

2022–2023 — Northern Kenya

Cattle Herder Insurance

Working with Fortune Credit and DIVA Protocol, Shamba provided parametric insurance coverage to 150 cattle herders in Northern Kenya. Payouts were triggered automatically when vegetation levels (NDVI) fell below thresholds indicating livestock starvation risk — removing the need for manual claims assessment and enabling rapid disbursement during drought emergencies.

2025 — Kenya

MSME Rainfall Insurance

A groundbreaking pilot with Mercy Corps Ventures, BlockBima, Fortune Credit, and RiskShield that embeds parametric rainfall insurance directly into Fortune Credit's 5-day digital loans for MSMEs. When excess rainfall occurs, insurance automatically reduces the borrower's repayment obligation — no claims filing required. Multiple severity tiers ensure greater rainfall triggers greater loan coverage. Shamba feeds 30+ satellite and sensor data streams into the blockchain daily.

2022–2023 — Gatanga, Kenya

Smallholder Carbon Credits

In Gatanga, north of Nairobi, Shamba partnered with local NGO YARD (Youth Action for Rural Development) to help smallholder farming communities issue their own carbon credits from organically grown fruit trees. Using Shamba's dMRV infrastructure, avocado farmers can now generate verifiable carbon credits for international markets — creating a new revenue stream that previously required costly manual audits.
