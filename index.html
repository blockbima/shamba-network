The Solution

How Shamba Works

Three types of data providers independently verify climate data through multi-layer aggregation. No single point of failure. No central authority.

1

Source Data

Node operators fetch satellite data from NASA, ESA, NOAA, and JAXA via cloud-deployed SDKs. Ground truthers collect field data (geotagged photos, GPS, and measurements) using the Shamba mobile app.

NASA ESA NOAA JAXA Field Data
2

Verify & Aggregate

Each provider type feeds into its own aggregation service. Data validators (domain experts) review edge cases. A master aggregation contract produces the final verified result. Staking and slashing ensure accuracy.

3

Deliver On-Chain

Consensus-verified data is delivered to smart contracts on Arbitrum, Avalanche, Ethereum, Optimism, Polygon, and the XRPL EVM Sidechain, powering insurance payouts, carbon credits, and climate finance. Community members earn by staking with trusted providers.

Use Cases

Powering the Climate Economy

What can you build with verified, on-chain climate data?

Parametric Insurance

Automated insurance payouts triggered by satellite-verified climate events. When rainfall exceeds thresholds or vegetation drops to critical levels, smart contracts pay out instantly. No claims filing, no waiting, no middlemen. Currently protecting 3,000+ farmers across East Africa with 40% lower premiums and 90% faster settlement.

View Case Studies

Carbon Credits & Impact Credits

Report, validate, tokenize, and monetize ecological impact. Shamba's dMRV pipeline enables smallholder farmers to create carbon credits from sustainable practices, verified by satellite, issued on-chain, tradeable on carbon marketplaces. Eliminates the $30-50/hectare cost of manual field audits.

View Case Studies

Climate Finance & Green Bonds

Verifiable ecological data for green bonds, climate-linked loans, and impact investing. Lenders can automatically adjust terms based on satellite-verified environmental outcomes. Enables climate finance to flow directly to the communities creating ecological value.

Prediction Markets

Prediction markets grew to $63.5 billion in 2025 trading volume, and they need reliable, decentralized data feeds for climate events, crop yields, and environmental outcomes. Shamba provides tamper-proof resolution data that no single party can manipulate.

Conditional Donations

Donor funds held in smart contracts and released only when satellite data confirms communities are affected by climate events. Proven in the DIVA Donate pilot: USDT disbursed to drought-affected pastoralists when pasture conditions hit distress levels. Transparent, accountable, automated.

Roadmap

Path to Decentralization

Phase 1: Oct - Dec 2026

Design

Token economics design (including delegated staking model) and Oracle Node SDK v1 (alpha Docker container). Mobile app design for ground truthing.

Phase 2: Jan - Mar 2027

Build

Multi-layer aggregation contracts, staking and delegated staking contracts. SDK beta. Shamba mobile app alpha for ground truthing.

Phase 3: Apr - Jun 2027

Test

Private testnet with 10-30 node operators and initial ground truthing pilots. Validate multi-layer consensus and delegated staking.

Phase 4: Jul - Sep 2027

Harden

Public testnet. Security audit. Credibility scoring system. Data validation corps onboarding. Provider ranking dashboard.

Phase 5: Oct - Dec 2027

Prepare

Mainnet launch preparation. Final security audits, stress testing, and community onboarding.

Phase 6: Jan - Mar 2028

Launch

Mainnet launch with all three provider types operational. Initial pilot migration from centralized to decentralized infrastructure.
